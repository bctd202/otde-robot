# Roadmap

## Phase 1
Backend/frontend application, mock provider, migrated and seeded database, dashboard, basic liquidity levels, structured setup detection, lottery filtering, persisted demo journal, and basic seeded analytics.

## Phase 2
Configurable live data adapter, richer WebSocket updates, full lottery scoring, Black-Scholes scenario tables, alerts and analytics.

## Phase 3
Replay mode, full options backtesting when historical option data exists, advanced paper fills and weekly optimization reports.
